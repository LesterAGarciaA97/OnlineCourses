Introduction to packet tracer


Packet Tracer is an exciting network design, simulation and modelling tool that allows you to develop your skill set in networking, cybersecurity, and the Internet of Things (IoT). 
It allows you to model complex systems without the need for dedicated equipment. It is used across numerous Cisco Academy courses to help develop and assess the skill set necessary for 
successful completion of the course.

In this chapter, Packet Tracer is introduced and instructions are provided to allow you to download and install it.
