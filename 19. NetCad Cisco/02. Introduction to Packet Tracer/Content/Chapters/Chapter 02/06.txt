Device configuration


Once your network has been created, it is time to configure the devices and components. Packet Tracer has the capability to configure the different intermediate and end devices that make 
up your network. To access the configuration interface of any devices first click on the device that you wish to configure. A popup window will appear displaying a series of tabs. 
Different types of devices have different interfaces.
