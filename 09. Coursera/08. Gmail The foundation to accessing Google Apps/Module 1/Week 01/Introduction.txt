Gmail: The Foundation To Accessing Google Apps

Now we will create a Google account and set up Gmail that will enable access to Google's free apps and services.

We will accomplish this by completing each task in the project:

    Task 1: Set up Gmail - You will create your Google account and navigate to your Gmail.
    Task 2: Organize Gmail - You will neatly organize your Gmail into categories that will be displayed in the inbox tabs.
    Task 3: Create labels for emails and archive messages - You will learn how to create labels for your emails and clean up your inbox by archiving messages.
    Task 4: Create email signature and set a Gmail background theme. You will create your email signature which is appended at the bottom of your outgoing messages. 
            You will also set your Gmail background theme.
    Task 5: Use Google hangouts chat - You will learn how to use Google hangouts chat to foster communication within an organization and build social relationships.

While you are watching me work on each step, you will get a cloud desktop with all the required software pre-installed. This will allow you to follow along the 
instructions to complete the above mentioned tasks. After all, we learn best with active, hands-on learning
