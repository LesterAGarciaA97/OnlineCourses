Quiz Explanations: N95 Masks - Clinical Skills

#1 How should an N95 mask be put on and taken off?

    Put the bottom strap on first, and take the bottom strap off first
    Put the top strap on first, and take the top strap off first
    Put the bottom strap on first, and take the top strap off first
    Put the top strap on first, and take the bottom strap off first

Explanation: Always start with the bottom strap when putting on or taking off an N95 mask. This is especially important when taking the mask off, so you can avoid touching to front of the mask and contaminating your hands with captured air particles.
